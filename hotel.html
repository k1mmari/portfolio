Modern Hotel Website

Role and Scope

Semester-long team project with three developers. I led the overall product design, user flow, and visual direction, defining the end-to-end experience.

Design Direction

A minimalist, modern interface designed to evoke a sense of luxury and high-end travel. Dark tones paired with high-contrast white elements create clarity while reinforcing a premium aesthetic. Content is organized into clearly defined cards to support structure and scannability.

UX Approach

The UX follows familiar booking patterns, intentionally simplified to reduce complexity and support lightweight implementation using vanilla HTML and CSS.
