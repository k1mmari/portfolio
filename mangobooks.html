MangoBooks

overview brand UI UX

A CPA firm website designed to provide clear information about services, contact details, and client resources, while establishing trust and professionalism for prospective and existing clients.

*names, phones, maps were omitted for privacy

My Role

I led the full redesign of the website, including visual design and information architecture. The project was completed within a two-week timeline to meet the client’s request for a rapid turnaround.

Target Audience

Aimed at individuals and small business owners seeking accounting services, with a focus on users who are not financially or technically specialized and need clear, trustworthy information.

Problem Statement

The previous website did not align with the firm’s brand, presenting a dark, overly technical visual style and a confusing information structure that made it difficult for users to navigate and understand services.
