The Medical Appointment Scheduling System streamlines clinic operations by connecting staff, doctors, and patients in one platform.

My Role

This was a semester-long academic project completed by a team of three developers. The overall scope and core functionality were defined by the professor. My primary responsibilities focused on UI/UX design and responsive implementation across devices.

Target Audience

Aimed at users who are discouraged by rigid, overwhelming medical systems and prefer a calmer, more approachable experience.

Problem Statement

Medical systems are often complex and friction-heavy. The goal of this project was to create an experience simple enough to remove unnecessary tension, especially in a medical context where users naturally approach the product with concern or anxiety.
