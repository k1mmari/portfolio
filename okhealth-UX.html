Onboarding

While the system does not strictly require a dedicated onboarding flow, it was designed to be accessible to users of varying ages and levels of computer literacy. Given the formal context of a medical application, a light onboarding layer provides reassurance and orientation without adding unnecessary friction.

The onboarding flow introduces the appâ€™s core navigation logic, which serves as the primary interaction point for users. Selecting a navigation tab updates the content panel on the right, helping users quickly understand how information is structured. The onboarding flow remains fully skippable for users who do not require additional guidance.

No Wheel Reinventing

To minimize friction, the interface follows established conventions for element placement. Core components such as the navigation bar and calendar remain persistently visible, ensuring quick access at all times. The AI chatbot is positioned at the bottom of the interface, aligning with common web patterns and user expectations.
