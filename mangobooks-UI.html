Consistency

While the logo and website mismatch had already been identified, the overall visual cohesiveness of the site was also fragmented. One major issue was a pop-up prompt that used an aggressively white background, which disrupted the otherwise dark, sleek visual language of the site. I redesigned the pop-up to align with the darker theme, creating a more cohesive and uninterrupted visual experience while maintaining clarity and usability.
