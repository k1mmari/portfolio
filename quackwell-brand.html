Quackwell

overview brand UI UX
overview brand UI UX

The app relies heavily on its mascot for user retention and for reflecting progress and regression. Due to limited resources, stock animations were not viable, making a custom mascot both practical and intentional.

The mascot needed to remain simple for scalability while still being visually distinct. Because the target audience skewed younger, the design taps into meme culture—specifically drawing inspiration from the “duck with a knife” meme—to create a playful, instantly recognizable character. Quackwell’s personality is intentionally slightly sassy, inspired by the Duolingo owl, as this tone has proven effective in maintaining long-term user motivation. The use of a mascot is also supported by established research, including the Tamagotchi Effect (Reeves & Nass, 1996) and Parasocial Interaction Theory (Horton & Wohl, 1956).Overall, the branding aims to keep the experience conversational and light, positioning the app as a friendly companion rather than a system enforcing habits.

Mascot Extension

I designed character stickers as a brand extension of Quackwell, reinforcing the app’s playful, supportive tone and strengthening emotional connection with the virtual pet.
